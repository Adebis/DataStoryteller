Story parts:

1. Story Curve Explanation - Tell them about the type of story curve that's
going to be used.
2. Domain Explanation - Tell them about what the measurement they're going to
look at is, as well as what direction of data change is considered Good and Bad.=
3. Data Explanation - Tell them about the data itself.
	- If standard, just tell them what the data does directly.
	- If Storytelling, use story curve and appropriate language.
